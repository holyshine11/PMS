1. Remove `final` from `kiccApiClient` and `kiccProperties` fields

   - Keep `@RequiredArgsConstructor` but mark `kiccApiClient` and `kiccProperties` with `@Autowired(required = false)` using field injection (NOT constructor injection) by removing `final` from those two fields and adding `@Autowired(required = false)` annotations directly on them.
